Common questions

Everything you need to know about KANAP — licensing, pricing, hosting, and Plaid.

What is the O'Saasy license?

It's an MIT-based open-source-style license that lets you inspect, modify, and self-host the code freely. The only restriction: you can't use it to compete directly by offering KANAP as your own SaaS product. Learn more about O'Saasy.

How does the free trial work?

Every paid cloud plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period, plus one free 60-minute activation session per company if booked during the trial.

What happens after my trial?

After your 14-day trial, you must choose a paid plan to continue. Your tenant will remain available for 30 more days with limited access. After this 30-day period your tenant will be deleted.

What is the free trial activation session?

Each trial includes one free 60-minute activation session per company. After subscription, we'll send you an email to book the call. The session happens over video call and is focused on first-value milestones based on your main objectives.

What's the difference between cloud and self-hosted?

Cloud hosting means we run everything for you — updates, backups, infrastructure. Self-hosted means you run KANAP on your own servers. Paid cloud plans (Starter, Standard, Max) include cloud hosting; the free tier is self-hosted only. You can purchase Self-Hosted Support for self-hosted installations.

What is Self-Hosted Support?

Self-Hosted Support is a professional support add-on for self-hosted installations. It includes priority email support and a 20% discount on consulting services. Priced at €2,490/yr.

How does priority support work?

For paying subscribers, email us about operational issues and we'll respond within 24h and fix your problem. It's best-effort, no SLA — but we're real humans who actually read and respond to every message. We'll get it done!

Can I pay by invoice?

Invoice payment (bank transfer) is available for subscriptions above €1,000 for EUR customers only. Today, that means Standard annual, Max annual, and Self-Hosted Support. Subscriptions at or below €1,000 are paid by card. Invoices are NET30.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. Switch between Starter, Standard, and Max anytime from your workspace settings. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged the prorated difference. Downgrade at the end of your billing period.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Of course. Simply cancel from your Billing Center at any time — your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, no questions asked.

How can I control Plaid?

At platform level, Plaid can be fully disabled, enabled in read-only mode, or enabled in read-write mode (with preview and confirmation for all changes). Web search and MCP can be enabled or disabled separately. At user level, you control who has access to which Plaid feature through role-based permissions. RBAC is always enforced — Plaid never sees more than the user is allowed to.
