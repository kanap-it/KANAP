KANAP is an online IT management platform designed for technology leaders and IT departments. The Platform provides tools for:

  • CAPEX and OPEX tracking and management
  • Multi-year budget planning and budget operations
  • Cost allocation to companies, departments, and business units
  • Application portfolio management with lifecycle tracking
  • Interface and integration documentation
  • Infrastructure registry and architecture visualization
  • Project portfolio management with capacity planning
  • Multi-tenant and multi-company support

The specific features of the Service are those described on the website at the time of subscription. The Platform is accessible via the internet and requires an internet connection.
