The Complete IT Toolbox

Three integrated modules.
One source of truth.

KANAP combines budget management, enterprise architecture documentation, and project portfolio management into one platform your entire IT department can use, with automatic roadmap scheduling built in.

Module 1

Budget Management

Master your IT budget with multi-year planning, intelligent cost allocations, and automated chargeback reports. Track OPEX and CAPEX with complete visibility across companies and departments.

  • Multi-year budget planning with dynamic columns
  • Six allocation methods including headcount and turnover
  • Multi-currency support with automatic FX rates
  • Executive chargeback reports by company and department
  • Freeze/unfreeze workflows for governance
Explore Budget Management

Module 2

IT Operations

Document your entire information system: applications, interfaces, and infrastructure. Visualize your architecture with interactive maps and track application lifecycles from proposal to retirement.

  • Application portfolio with per-environment instances
  • Interface documentation with 3-leg middleware support
  • Server and infrastructure registry
  • Interactive Interface Map and Connection Map
  • Version lineage tracking for application evolution
Explore IT Operations

Module 3

Portfolio Management

From request to delivery: prioritize demand, simulate feasible delivery plans, and apply roadmap dates with confidence. Make data-driven decisions about what to build and when.

  • Configurable request scoring with weighted criteria
  • Request to project conversion workflow
  • Automatic roadmap generation from effort, dependencies, and capacity
  • Bottleneck and occupation analysis by contributor and team
  • Selective transactional apply of generated dates
  • Baseline capture for variance analysis
  • Team collaboration with activity history
Explore Portfolio Management
