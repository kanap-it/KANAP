What KANAP offers

Three essential capabilities for IT management, natively integrated in a single platform.

Portfolio Management

Request intake, prioritization scoring, CAB workflows, project tracking, Kanban and timeline views.

Enterprise Architecture

Budget Management

Cost tracking by project, application, and department. Internal invoicing, chargeback, multi-company allocation.

Native integration makes the difference

How KANAP compares to common alternatives

IT teams typically evaluate KANAP alongside these categories of tools.

vs. Enterprise Architecture Platforms

They excel at large-scale EA governance but typically cost 20,000–150,000+ EUR/year and don't include budget or portfolio management. Platforms like Planview, Clarity PPM, or ServiceNow SPM offer deep portfolio capabilities for large enterprises. Many IT teams run on Excel, Google Sheets, Jira, and Confluence. These are flexible and familiar, but data lives in silos, version control is manual, and there's no single source of truth. KANAP replaces the patchwork with one integrated platform purpose-built for IT departments.

KANAP's budget module provides multi-year planning, cost allocation, and chargeback reports natively integrated with your application portfolio and project pipeline — without enterprise pricing or complexity.
