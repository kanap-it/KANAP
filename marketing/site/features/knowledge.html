Knowledge

Your IT documentation, connected to everything

Markdown-based document governance with structured libraries, review workflows, and deep integration with your applications, assets, projects, and tasks.

Markdown Editor with Governance

Write and maintain IT documentation with a rich markdown editor. Edit locks prevent concurrent modifications, and autosave ensures nothing is lost. Embed inline images and format content with full markdown support.

  • Rich markdown editor with live preview
  • Edit locks to prevent concurrent modifications
  • Autosave with manual save option
  • Inline image support
  • Full markdown formatting: headings, lists, tables, code blocks

Libraries, Folders & Document Types

Organize your documentation into libraries, browse by folder, and classify documents by type. Use templates to kickstart new documents with predefined structure and content.

  • Multiple libraries for different knowledge domains
  • Folder hierarchy for logical organization
  • Document types for classification and governance
  • Templates grouped by type for reusable starters
  • Browse, search, and filter across all documents

Review & Approval Workflows

Assign owners, authors, reviewers, and approvers to documents. Submit documents for review, collect decision notes, and track approval status. Editing is blocked during the review process to maintain integrity.

  • Owner, author, reviewer, and approver roles
  • Submit for review with one click
  • Decision notes from reviewers and approvers
  • Editing blocked during active review
  • Approval status tracking and history

Connected Documentation

Link documents to applications, assets, projects, requests, and tasks. Access managed documents from other workspaces and distinguish between linked and related content for full traceability across your IT landscape.

  • Relations to applications, assets, projects, requests, and tasks
  • Managed documents accessible from other workspaces
  • Linked vs related distinction for precise traceability
  • Bidirectional navigation between documents and linked entities
  • Full audit trail of relation changes
