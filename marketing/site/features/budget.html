Budget Management

Master your IT budget with complete visibility

Multi-year planning, intelligent cost allocations, and executive-ready chargeback reports. Track OPEX and CAPEX across companies and departments.

Multi-Year Budget Planning

Plan your IT budget across multiple years with dynamic columns for Budget, Revision, Follow-up, and Landing. Track both OPEX (operational expenses) and CAPEX (capital investments) in one unified view.

  • OPEX and CAPEX tracking in dedicated grids
  • Dynamic columns: Budget, Revision, Follow-up, Landing per year
  • Year-over-year comparisons and trend analysis
  • Bulk copy operations between budget years
  • Freeze/unfreeze workflows for governance

Intelligent Cost Allocation

Distribute IT costs across companies and departments using six different allocation methods. The system automatically recomputes allocations when metrics change, maintaining full transparency and audit trails.

  • Headcount: Allocate based on employee count
  • IT Users: Distribute by IT user count
  • Turnover: Weight by company revenue
  • Manual Company: Set custom company percentages
  • Manual Department: Define department-level splits
  • Default: Use proportional headcount

Multi-Currency Support

Work with multiple currencies while reporting in a single canonical currency. KANAP automatically fetches exchange rates from the World Bank and captures frozen rate snapshots when you lock budget versions.

  • Single reporting currency for all aggregates
  • Automatic FX rates from World Bank API
  • Frozen rate snapshots for budget versions
  • Configurable allowed currency list
  • Historical rates for past fiscal years

Executive Chargeback Reports

Generate board-ready chargeback reports showing IT cost distribution by company and department. Drill down from company totals to individual cost items with full transparency on allocation methods.

  • Global chargeback report by company
  • Company-level reports by department
  • Itemized allocation breakdowns
  • KPI metrics and share of total
  • CSV export and chart downloads
